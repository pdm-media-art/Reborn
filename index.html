From Darkness to Light

I am a survivor of childhood sexual abuse. For over 15 years, I battled pornography addiction so severe it nearly destroyed my marriage, my mental health, and my will to live.

Today, I'm 5 years free from pornography. My marriage is stronger than ever. I've reconnected with my authentic self and discovered what real masculinity means.

If I can do it, so can you. Let me show you how.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting

How long does it take to see results?

Every journey is different, but most clients report significant changes within the first 30-60 days when they fully commit to the process. The key is consistency and following the 7-step method.

Is this program confidential?

Absolutely. All coaching sessions, communications, and personal information are kept strictly confidential. Your privacy and trust are paramount.

What if I've tried everything and failed before?

I tried everything too - therapy, willpower, support groups. What makes this different is addressing the root cause (trauma) and rewiring your brain at a neurological level. This isn't about willpower; it's about transformation.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! Payment plans are available for the Write Your Story program, coaching packages, and the Mastermind Bundle. Contact me to discuss options that work for your situation.

What makes your approach different?

I've lived this. I'm not a therapist who read about addiction in a textbook. I battled 15+ years of pornography addiction stemming from childhood sexual abuse. The 7-step method comes from real experience, real pain, and real freedom.

Can this work for me if I don't have childhood trauma?

Yes! While my story includes trauma, the 7-step method works for anyone struggling with pornography addiction, regardless of the root cause. The principles of brain rewiring, habit change, and holistic transformation apply universally.
