About Patrick Devosse

From Darkness to Light

I am a survivor of childhood sexual abuse. For over 15 years, I battled pornography addiction so severe it nearly destroyed my marriage, my mental health, and my will to live.

I sat on a couch with a weapon in hand, ready to end it all. But I made a different choice. I chose to heal instead of to die.

This book and these programs aren't based on theory. They're based on lived experience - the darkest nights and the hard-won victories. I'm not a therapist or psychologist. I'm a man who found a way out and now dedicates his life to showing others the path.

Today, I'm nearly 2 years free from pornography. My marriage is stronger than ever. I've reconnected with my authentic self and discovered what real masculinity means.

If I can do it, so can you. Let me show you how.
