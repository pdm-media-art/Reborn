REBORN Method — Phase 1: DESTROY

Trigger Map
Workbook

You can't fight what you can't see. This workbook helps you map every trigger that leads to unwanted behavior — the times, places, emotions, and thought patterns that fuel the urge. Once you can see the full chain, you can break it.

How to Use This Workbook

  • Work through all sections in order — they build on each other
  • Be brutally honest — no one sees this but you and Patrick
  • Fill it out after a urge or relapse while it's still fresh
  • Review it with Patrick in your next session
  • The goal is awareness, not shame — every map brings you closer to free

Your Trigger Categories

What situations consistently lead to urges? Write them in the boxes below.

The Dopamine Trap: Your brain has wired a direct highway from certain situations to the craving. The highway was built through thousands of repetitions. You can't delete the highway — but you can build a detour. That starts with knowing exactly where the on-ramps are.

⏰ Times & Routines

e.g., late at night, after work, weekend mornings, lunch break alone

📍 Places & Devices

e.g., bedroom with phone, bathroom, laptop alone at home, hotel rooms

😔 Emotional States

e.g., boredom, loneliness, stress, rejection, anxiety, feeling "numb"

💭 Thought Patterns

e.g., "just once," "I deserve this," "no one will know," "tomorrow I'll stop"

👥 Social Situations

e.g., after a fight, when feeling left out, after social events, isolation

🌐 Digital Triggers

e.g., certain apps, Instagram, YouTube rabbit holes, late-night scrolling

For each type of trigger, define your personal interrupt. Make it specific — vague plans fail.

Replace, Don't Just Resist: Willpower is a limited resource. The brain needs to go somewhere when a trigger fires. Pre-load the destination so you don't have to think in the moment.
