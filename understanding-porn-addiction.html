Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Phase 1 and Phase 2?

Phase 1 (DESTROY) is about stopping the behavior. Phase 2 (UNDERSTANDING) is about understanding why it started. Without understanding, most men relapse within weeks because they haven't addressed the root cause.

Do I need to have childhood trauma to be addicted to porn?

Not necessarily — but most men who develop problematic pornography use have unprocessed emotional pain from some period of their life. This doesn't have to be dramatic abuse. It can be emotional neglect, loneliness, or a specific moment of pain the brain decided to avoid.

What is "The Addiction Birthday"?

Your Addiction Birthday is the moment your brain first connected pornography with emotional relief. It's usually a specific age or event. Finding it is one of the most powerful steps in Phase 2 — because it explains everything.

How does dopamine relate to porn addiction?

Pornography produces one of the highest dopamine spikes possible — comparable to hard drugs. Over time, your brain needs more stimulation to feel the same effect, and normal life activities feel flat. Understanding this process is the first step to reversing it.
